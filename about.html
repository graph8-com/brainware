Our Story

Founded in 2020, Brainware began with a simple yet powerful vision: to unlock the full potential of voice data through artificial intelligence.

Our founders recognized that voice contains immense untapped insights that could transform how businesses understand their customers, operations, and opportunities. What started as a research project quickly evolved into a comprehensive voice intelligence platform used by organizations worldwide.

Today, Brainware is at the forefront of voice AI technology, continuously innovating to help our clients harness the power of voice data to drive better decisions, improve experiences, and solve complex challenges.
